About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Mechanical Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. I work on microfluidics, electrohydrodynamics, and subsurface hydrology. Various aspects of automation and robotics also interest me.

Prior to joining IIT Kharagpur, I was a postdoctoral researcher in Geosciences Rennes (CNRS-University of Rennes 1), France and an Alexander von Humboldt fellow at TU Darmstadt, Germany. Before that, I obtained my Dual degree and PhD from IIT Kharagpur.
